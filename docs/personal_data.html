Personal Data

To be in control of your personal data, you have the following rights:
- Accessing / reviewing / updating / correcting your personal data. You may request a copy of, as well as updating and correcting your personal data collected during your use of the Service at support@bsnj.co.
- Deleting your personal data. You can request erasure of your personal data by sending us an email at support@bsnj.co.
- When you request deletion of your personal data, we will use reasonable efforts to honor your request. In some cases we may be legally required to keep some of the data for a certain time; in such event, we will fulfill your request after we have complied with our obligations.
